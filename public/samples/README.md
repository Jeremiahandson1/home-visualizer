# Sample House Photos

Place sample house photos here for the "Try with a sample" feature on the upload screen.

Required files:
- `ranch.jpg` — Single-story ranch home, front view
- `colonial.jpg` — Two-story colonial home, front view  
- `modern.jpg` — Modern/contemporary home, front view

Requirements:
- JPG format, 1024x768 or larger
- Clear front-facing exterior photo
- Good lighting, no obstructions
- You MUST have rights to use these images (stock photos with commercial license, or your own photos)

The sample photo buttons will silently not appear if these files are missing.
